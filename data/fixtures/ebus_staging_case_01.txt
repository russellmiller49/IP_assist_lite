EBUS systematic staging via ETT for suspected lung cancer.
Stations sampled: 4R (12mm, oval, homogeneous, elastography blue/stiff), 
7 (8mm, round, heterogeneous, elastography heterogeneous), 
11R (15mm, irregular, heterogeneous, elastography blue/stiff).
All stations sampled with 22G needle, 3 passes each.
ROSE adequate for lymphoid tissue at all stations.
Also performed BAL RML, 100mL instilled, 45mL returned, sent for cytology and micro.
No complications. EBL minimal.
