Percutaneous dilatational tracheostomy performed in ICU for prolonged mechanical ventilation.
Bronchoscopic guidance throughout procedure. Identified tracheal rings, puncture between rings 2-3.
Needle entry confirmed under direct visualization. Sequential dilations 8Fr to 36Fr performed.
Used Ciaglia Blue Rhino kit. Size 8 Shiley tracheostomy tube placed.
ETT withdrawn to subglottic position then removed after cuff inflation.
Final bronchoscopic check: tube tip 4cm above carina, bilateral ventilation confirmed.
No bleeding, no pneumothorax, no loss of airway. EBL < 10mL.
